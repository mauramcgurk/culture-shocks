We improve the cultural experience of exchange students

About

Conventional wisdom says that studying abroad is like living a fairy tale...making friends all over the world...learning a new language...experiencing romance in an exotic locale...

No matter how beautiful the surroundings, or the warmth of the host family, the reality of studying abroad is one of being far from the customary support network, without a fluent understanding of the local language and customs, without the comforts and foods of home. It often comes as a surprise that there is a second adjustment period that occurs after reintegration in the home culture, when customs, support systems, etc are again disrupted.

Culture Shocks is an app designed by exchange students, for exchange students, to help them deal with the stresses of living abroad.
